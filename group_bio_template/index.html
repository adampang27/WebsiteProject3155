Our Mission Statement

We are The Dream Team — a group of computer science students who value teamwork, creativity, and learning. Our mission is to work together to build useful and meaningful technology. We support each other, solve problems as a team, and aim to keep growing our skills along the way.

Team Rules

  • Respect everyone’s ideas and listen to each other.
  • Communicate clearly and respond on time.
  • Help each other when someone needs support.
  • Be open to learning new things and accepting feedback.
  • Stay focused and work toward our common goals.
